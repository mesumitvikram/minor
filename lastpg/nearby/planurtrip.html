Plan Your Trip To Nalanda

Rajgir - A Sacred Destination

Travel Information

  • Nalanda is accessible by road, rail, and air.
  • Nearest railway station: Biharsharif Junction(~15km)
  • Closest airport: Lok Nayak Jayaprakash Airport (PAT)(~80KM) / Gaya international airport (~80km)

Local Travel Information

  • e-ricksaw or local buses are aviailable there
  • tumtum is the ancient one

Costing and Budgeting

  • Accommodation: 4*,3* and 2* Hotels are easily available
  • Attractions: Some attractions may have entry fees; plan accordingly.

Attractions and Activities

  • Vishwa Shanti Stupa
  • Nalanda University Ruins
  • Rajgir Hot Springs
  • Venu Vana
  • Ghora Katora

Travel Tips

  • Visit in the period of september-march for your best experience
  • Packing: Pack clothes accordingly based on the season.
  • Cultural Etiquette: Be aware of local customs and norms.

Emergency Info

  • Police Helpline no: 112 (Applicable for all regions of Bihar State)
  • Nearby Hospital: Jeevan Jyoti Superspeciality(pvt.), VIMS(Govt),Local primary healthcare system available
  • Locals are so friendly so in any emergency you can interact with them
